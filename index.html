About Website Founder & Outlook for Collaborators

Yun, known professionally as Yunyao Gu, is a humble, hungry, and foolish lifelong learner and business starter. She is the Business Owner of Divine Design and a former senior UX/UI designer and design consultant with 4 years of full-time experience across technology, education, e-commerce, insurance, and finance sectors.

At the helm of Divine Design, a socially responsible design and strategy company, Yun prioritizes human-centered values, locality, and iterative processes over profit-driven motives. She brings expertise in rapid prototyping, full-stack UCD methodology, and stakeholder management, consistently delivering thoughtful, impactful work.

Yun holds an MBA from Quantic School of Business (scholarship recipient) and completed the one-year LEAD Online Business Program at Stanford Graduate School of Business.
